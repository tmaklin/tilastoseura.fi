The 6th Baltic-Nordic Conference on Survey Statistics

Helsinki, 21-25 August 2023; see: https://wiki.helsinki.fi/display/BNU/BANOCOSS2023.

Call for papers is open (deadline: 30 April 2023)

The 6th Baltic-Nordic Conference on Survey Statistics provides an international forum for sharing new results and ideas in survey statistics and data science research and application. The conference is organized by the Baltic-Nordic-Ukrainian Network on Survey Statistics and University of Helsinki. For more information see the conference website: https://wiki.helsinki.fi/display/BNU/BANOCOSS2023.
