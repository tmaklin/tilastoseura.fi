Proceedings of the 2024 annual meeting

The annual meeting of the Statistical Society was held on February 29, 2024. At the meeting, a new board was elected for the statistical society with the following composition:

  • Pekka Pere (chairman)
  • Ari Jaakola (vice-chairman)
  • Roope Rihtamo (secretary)
  • Henrik Törmi (treasurer)
  • Arvi Tolvanen
  • Reija Helenius
  • Mika Gissler
  • Jyrki Möttönen
  • Tommi Härkänen
  • Elisa Falck (deputy member)
  • Tapani Linnaluoto (deputy member)
  • Kari Djerf (deputy member)
  • Arvi Tolvanen (master of ceremonies)

The term of office of the new board lasts until the next annual meeting, which will be held in February 2025.
