The next ECAS-SFdS school will take place from October 8 to 13, 2023, on the French Riviera. The course entitled "Random forests: basics, extensions and applications", will present, starting from the basics on tree-based methods and popular tree ensemble methods, several extensions of random forests and will demonstrate their ability in applications coming from several domains. The link between random forests and other popular machine learning techniques will also be discussed, as well as some clues about the use of some more abstract tools to analyse the behavior of random forests.
