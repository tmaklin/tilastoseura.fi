Nordic-Baltic Biometric Conference 2023 will be held in Gothenburg on June 16-18

Warmly welcome to the Nordic-Baltic Biometric Conference 2023 which will be held in Gothenburg June 16-18 (the weekend before NORDSTAT). There will be key note speakers, invited sessions and contributed sessions. The three key note speakers as well as the organizers of the five invited sessions are all very knowledgeable persons so we can definitely look forward to three interesting days. We are also sure that many interesting contributions will appear. Moreover, there will be plenty of time for meeting old friends and creating new contacts during the welcome reception, conference dinner, health sessions and lunches which except the conference dinner will take place at the Scandic Crown hotel.

For further information please visit https://ibsnbr.org/NBBC23/ (which is weekly updated).
