Statistical Days 2024

Information about the conference

The next Statistical Days will be held on Monday 3 June 2024 at Aalto University in Espoo. The keynote speaker is Professor Ryan Martin (Department of Statistics, North Carolina State University).

The Statistical Society offers coffee during Statistical Days. Participants pay for their own lunch.

Monday 3 June 2024

Conference venue: Aalto-yliopisto, Kandidaattikeskus, Otakaari 1, C-sali (2-kerros)

Poster competition

A poster competition is organized in connection with the Statistical Days. Sign up for the poster competition upon registration. Send the information about the title of the poster and the authors by e-mail to the secretary of the Statistical Society () . The size of the poster stand is 79 cm (width) x 119 cm (height), so it can hold an A0-sized poster.

Research showcase

Statistical Days has a new event "Research Showcase". It consists of three presentations (20 min. + discussion 10 min.). The Statistical Society awards the presenters with an honorary certificate. Presentations must deal with high-level Finnish research recently published or accepted for publication in a way that must be understood by statisticians other than those specializing in the field.
