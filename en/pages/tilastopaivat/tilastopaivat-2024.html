Statistical Days 2024 (advance information)

The next Statistical Days will be held on Monday 3 June 2024 at Aalto University in Espoo. The main speaker is Professor Ryan Martin (Department of Statistics, North Carolina State University).

The Finnish Statistical Society offers coffee during the Statistical Days. Participants pay for their own lunch. The conference dinner organized on Monday evening is included in the participation fee.
